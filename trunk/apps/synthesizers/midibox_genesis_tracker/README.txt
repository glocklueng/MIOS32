Required hardware:
   o MBHP_CORE_STM32F4
   o Zero, one, or two MIDI_IO modules
   o MBHP_Genesis (http://wiki.midibox.org/doku.php?id=mbhp_genesis)
   o MBHP_Genesis_LS (http://wiki.midibox.org/doku.php?id=mbhp_genesis_ls)
   o No LCD or DIN/DOUT

===============================================================================

This application controls the OPN2 and PSG sound chips on the MBHP_Genesis
board via MIDI messages. No synth engine features or other controls are
provided.
